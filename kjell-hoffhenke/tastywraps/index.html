Tasty Wraps

Ingredients (as preferred)

  • Warmed tortilla wraps
  • Curry sauce
  • Dressing, fe sylter or joghurt
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Chicken or turkey breast
  • Red onions
  • Corn
  • Paprika
  • Other vegtables and ingredients as desired, fe replace meat with avocado or tofu

Preparation

  1. Cut all the tomatoes, cucumbers, onions and other vegtables into small blocks
  2. Cook the chicken or turkey and cut it up aswell
  3. Quickly heat up the tortilla wraps in a pan (one by one, they cool down fast)
  4. Spread the curry sauce onto the tortilla wraps
  5. Add ingridients as desired
  6. Finish it off with the dressing ontop
  7. Fold and roll up the wraps (see below)
  8. Enjoy!
